Sign in to Microsoft

This app needs your permission to add pages to your OneNote. Sign in with the Microsoft account your OneNote is on — a personal account (Outlook.com, Hotmail) or a work or school account.

Opening your web browser for sign-in…

Sign in there and this app continues automatically. If the browser didn't open, or you closed it, click Cancel and try again.

Choose your Evernote export

In Evernote, select your notebook and choose File → Export Notes, saving as an .enex file. Then pick that file here.
